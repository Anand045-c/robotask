Join the Robotics Club!

Hi, I’m Anand, the Secretary of the Robotics Club.

When I first joined college, I was deeply curious about how robots work and what goes into building them. That curiosity led me to explore the basics, and along the way, I connected with some amazing people from this club. That one step changed everything—being part of the Robotics Club gave my journey the direction, support, and momentum it needed.

Whether I was stuck on a concept or looking for guidance on a project, this community was always there to help. Now, as the Secretary, I’ve had the privilege to work on several exciting projects and, more importantly, to give back by supporting others on their own robotics journeys.

If you're passionate about innovation, problem-solving, or simply want to learn something new, I genuinely believe you'll find the Robotics Club to be as inspiring and helpful as I did. Welcome aboard!
